Minimal Launcher ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our Android application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Our core philosophy is minimalism and privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, contacts, location, or private files to any external servers. All core app functionalities are performed locally on your device.

2. Usage Access Permission (Screen Time)

To provide features such as App Limits and Focus Modes, Minimal Launcher requires the Usage Access permission. This permission allows the app to monitor which apps are currently running and calculate your daily screen time.

  • Local Processing: This data is processed strictly locally on your device.
  • No External Transmission: We NEVER transmit your screen time data, usage history, or app lists to our servers or any third-party servers.

3. Third-Party Services (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements within the app (for non-premium users). Google AdMob may collect and use data such as your device's advertising ID, IP address, and general usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

For more information on how Google uses this data, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

Because we do not transmit your personal data to our servers, your data remains as secure as your device. Your custom app limits, themes, and settings are stored locally within the app's secure storage provided by the Android OS.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the developer email provided on our Google Play Store listing.
