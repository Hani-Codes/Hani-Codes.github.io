Privacy Policy for GPS & Activity Tracking Applications

Effective Date: May 2026 | Last Updated: May 18, 2026

English Version

Welcome to our Activity & GPS Tracking Mobile Applications. Your privacy and digital security are our top priorities. This universal privacy policy applies to all our mobile applications that utilize location services and physical activity tracking.

📍 Location Data & GPS Permissions

How We Use Location: Our tracking applications request fine and coarse location access (GPS coordinates) strictly during active recording sessions. This access is required to accurately calculate distance traveled, trajectory routes, speed, and real-time pace for your personal fitness and activity logs.

100% Local Data Processing: All location data, geographic coordinates, and calculated metrics are processed and stored strictly locally on your physical device within the app's secure internal database (SQLite / Room).

Zero External Transmission: We do NOT transmit your live or historical location data to any external cloud servers. We do NOT track you in the background when sessions are stopped. Furthermore, we do NOT share, sell, lease, or distribute your personal location data or activity records to any third-party advertisers or external entities under any circumstances.

User Profiles & Data Deletion

All recorded profiles, user settings, active session logs, and accumulated stats remain locally encrypted on your mobile device. You maintain absolute control over your data. You may permanently erase all stored records at any time directly through the in-app settings or by uninstalling the application from your operating system.

Contact Information

If you have any formal questions, GDPR inquiries, or technical support requests regarding our privacy standards across any of our tracking apps, please contact developer Hani Ahmed directly at: hani.ahmed209@gmail.com
