Six Pack & Belly Fat: 30 Days

Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy details how the Six Pack & Belly Fat: 30 Days mobile application handles your personal data, permissions, and advertising. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Data Collection: What We Do NOT Collect

We believe in total privacy. We do not run any cloud servers, databases, or tracking pipelines. Your workouts are your business.

  • No Personal Information: We do not ask for or collect your name, email, phone number, gender, or age.
  • No Account Registration: No account creation is required to use our services.
  • No Cloud Storage: All weight logs, workout progress, streaks, and hydration tracking are stored strictly locally on your physical device.

Local Device Storage

To provide a functional and gamified training plan, we store the following data locally on your device (using SharedPreferences):

  • Completed Days: Tracking which of the 30 workout days you have completed.
  • Streak & Freezes: Keeping track of your daily consecutive workout streak and remaining streak freezes.
  • Weight Logs: Storing dates and weights you enter locally to draw the progress chart.
  • Water Tracker: Saving your daily water intake count for hydration goals.

*Uninstalling the application will permanently erase all local storage and reset your progress, as no backups are uploaded to any external servers.

Google AdMob & Third-Party Services

Our app displays advertisements powered by Google AdMob to keep our training plan 100% free for everyone. AdMob may utilize third-party SDKs to collect anonymous identifiers to deliver personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

  • Advertising ID: AdMob may collect your device's Mobile Advertising ID (e.g., Google ADID) to display ads. You can opt-out or reset your ID inside your phone's Android System Settings.
  • Device Diagnostics: Basic, non-personal system information (device model, OS version, app crashes) is gathered by AdMob to measure performance and delivery.

Required App Permissions

To deliver modern features like background voice coaching, daily reminder notifications, and streak safety checks, the app requests the following system permissions:

POST_NOTIFICATIONS
Used to display the daily reminder alerts to complete your workout.
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED
Reschedules your scheduled daily reminders automatically when your phone reboots.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE
Ensures the workout player and Text-to-Speech voice coach operate smoothly without being killed in the background.
WAKE_LOCK
Keeps the timer active during your training intervals so the countdown doesn't freeze.
INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
Required to load advertisements from Google AdMob servers.
