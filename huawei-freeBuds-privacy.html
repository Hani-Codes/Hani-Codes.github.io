1. Introduction

Thank you for using Sync for Huawei FreeBuds. We respect your privacy and want to be transparent about how the app works. This page explains what data is (and is not) collected, what permissions are used, and how you can contact us if you have any questions.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal information

This app does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or exact geographical location. We also do not ask you to create an account or sign in with any external service.

2.2 Usage data & analytics

We do not use third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) inside the app. Any information about how you use the app stays on your device only and is not sent to our servers.

3. Device Permissions

To provide a seamless connection and management experience for your wireless earbuds, this application requires certain device permissions. We use these permissions solely for the functionality of the app and do not collect or share any personal information through them:

  • Bluetooth & Nearby Devices (android.permission.BLUETOOTH_CONNECT, BLUETOOTH_SCAN): Crucial for the core functionality of the app. It allows the app to securely scan for, identify, and establish a connection with your wireless earbuds via Bluetooth.
  • Precise Location (android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION): On older versions of Android (Android 11 and below), Google requires Location permission to scan for Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices. We do not track, process, or store your physical location.
  • Internet Access (android.permission.INTERNET): Allows the app to connect to the internet to request and display advertising (Google AdMob), helping keep this app free for everyone.

4. Third-Party Services

The app integrates third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to display ads within the application. These services may collect limited device information (such as device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data) for advertising and performance purposes. This data is processed directly by the advertising provider in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not have direct access to this data.

5. Data Security

Because the app does not transmit personal data to our servers, most of your information remains safely stored on your device. Please keep your device protected with a screen lock and up-to-date operating system to help maintain security.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will remove it if stored anywhere under our control.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or in legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. We recommend that you review this page periodically.
