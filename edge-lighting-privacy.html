Privacy Policy for Edge Lighting

Effective Date: July 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Edge Lighting application for mobile devices. We take your privacy very seriously. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and what permissions are required for the app to function properly.

1. Information We DO NOT Collect

We believe that your personal data is yours. Therefore:

  • We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.
  • We do not collect or read the content of your notifications or messages.
  • We do not collect your contacts, phone numbers, or call logs.
  • We do not use any remote servers to process or store your data. All features work locally on your device.

2. Permissions Required and Why

To provide our core features (animating your screen edges), Edge Lighting requires certain device permissions. Here is exactly how they are used:

Notification Access (BIND_NOTIFICATION_LISTENER_SERVICE)

This is a core permission required to trigger the edge lighting effect when you receive a new notification (e.g., WhatsApp, SMS, Instagram). We only detect the "arrival event" of a notification. We absolutely DO NOT read, store, or share the text, sender name, or any content of your notifications. The detection happens entirely locally on your phone.

Draw Over Other Apps (SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW)

This permission is required to draw the edge lighting animations on top of your screen, allowing the lights to be visible even when the app is closed or your phone is locked.

Read Phone State (READ_PHONE_STATE)

This permission is used solely to detect when there is an incoming call so the app can illuminate your screen edges. We do not record calls, read call history, or access your phone number.

3. Data Storage

Any settings or customizations you create within the app (such as colors, animation speed, notch types) are saved locally on your device using Android's standard preferences system. If you uninstall the app, this configuration data is automatically deleted.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
