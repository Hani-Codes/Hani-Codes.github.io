1. Introduction

Thank you for using GT6 Pro Tools. We respect your privacy and want to be fully transparent about how our companion app works. This policy explains what data is collected, how smartwatch syncing and health sensors operate, and how you can contact us if you have any questions.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

GT6 Pro Tools does not collect or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to our servers. We also do not ask you to create an external account or sign in with any cloud service.

2.2 Usage Data & Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) inside the app. Any information about how you use the app stays on your device only and is not sent to our servers.

3. Device Permissions & Sensor Access

3.1 Standard Permissions

The app requests standard permissions such as Bluetooth connection solely to pair with and manage your smartwatch hardware. We do not request microphone or camera access unless directly required by an active feature.

📍 3.2 Location Data (Car Locator Feature)

We request location permissions (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION and ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) solely to enable the 'Car Locator' feature, which allows you to save your parking spot directly from your watch. Your location data is used only for this purpose, is processed strictly locally on your device, and is never uploaded to any external servers or shared with third parties.

❤️ 3.3 Health Data (Google Health Connect)

Our app reads your step count data via Google Health Connect to calculate your dynamic hydration goals in the Water Tracker. This data is accessed only with your explicit permission, is processed locally on your device, and is not stored or transmitted anywhere outside your physical phone.

🔄 3.4 Local Data Processing & Notification Sync

All data handled by GT6 Pro Tools (including steps, location, and companion notification sync) is stored and processed strictly locally on your device. We do not use any external cloud storage or servers to store your personal or health data.

4. Third-Party Services

The app integrates third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to display ads within the application. These services may collect limited device information (such as device identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data) for advertising and performance purposes. This data is processed directly by the advertising provider in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not have direct access to this data.

5. Data Security

Because the app does not transmit personal or health data to our servers, most of your information remains safely stored on your device. Please keep your device protected with a screen lock and up-to-date operating system to help maintain security.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal or health information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will remove it if stored anywhere under our control.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or in legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. We recommend that you review this page periodically.
